Max Temperature

As expected, the temperature increases on one approaches the equator (0 Degrees Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of tne earth at the time of year this data was gathered.
